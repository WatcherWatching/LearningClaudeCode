Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, and the expense data you log within the application. This information is used solely to provide you with the expense tracking service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the service.
  • Generate spending reports and summaries.
  • Improve the functionality of the application.
  • Provide customer support.

3. Data Storage

We use secure servers to store your data. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the application, but these providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your data, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com.
